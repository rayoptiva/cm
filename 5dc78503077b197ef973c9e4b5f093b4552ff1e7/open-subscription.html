Viewing Subscriptions

Open the subscriptions page by clicking Subscriptions on the left navigation. View usage and balance details.

  1. Search by Account ID or BAN.
    • Click the advanced filter to search by specific options.
    • Click on the icon to configure the layout and display of columns in the search results.
  2. Filter your search by clicking the following filter icons:

    • Mobile
    • Landline
    • Ethernet
    • IPTV
