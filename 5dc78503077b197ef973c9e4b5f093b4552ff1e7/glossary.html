Glossary

Terms uses in the customer managment application.

Table 1. Glossary
Term Description
BAN Unique Billing Account Number (BAN)
Bundle A group of products that are sold together, that is, packaged.
Closed User Group (CUG) A CUG is a defined closed group that incorporates subscriptions to which users can be assigned. Users in the same group receive discounted rates when they call one another.
Group Account

An account that can contain responsible and non-responsible subscriptions. All the subscriptions, irrespective of the responsibility, can share all the benefits of a group account.
Group-Pooled Account

A pooled account is a group account containing multiple subscriptions. It is used to accrue all non-VPN transactions for the account against a single subscription.

Group-pooled accounts enable subscriptions to be billed to one account, appear on the same invoice (if the optional invoicing feature is supported), and share other account and billing properties.
Individual Account An individual subscriber account.
MSISDN MSISDN stands for Mobile Station International Subscriber Directory Number. It’s the unique number that identifies a mobile subscriber.
Overage Charge A charge for using over and above what was agreed.
Pooled Account An account where each subscription of the subscriber accounts in the hierarchy shares a single balance.
Responsible Account

An account that is responsible to pay for the services availed of by the account or sub-accounts.
Subscriber The individual associated with a Subscriber Account. It is represented by the personal information of an individual, whom the Operator subscribes for a subscription.
Subscriber Account An account opened in the name of an individual subscriber. It contains the billingi nformation of the subscriber and contains one or more subscriptions.
Subscription It represents the service offerings that an operator offers to its account holders. For example: GSM Mobile Phone, CDMA Mobile Phone, Mobile Wallet and so on. A subscription is created inside an account and is characterized by a Subscription Class.
Tax Authorities

Postpaid feature. Determines the tax rate (%) to apply postpaid subscriptions in an account.
Sales Tax in Canada
  • Provincial Sales Tax (PST)

    Provincial sales tax is a consumer tax. Businesses selling physical goods must pay PST and will charge this on their sales.

  • Goods and Services Tax (GST)

    GST is charged at a rate of 5% and is payable on purchases of goods and services in a Canada. Some parts of Canada pay HST, which combines GST and PST.
Wallet This is a type of subscription that allows vendors or subscribers to store funds in a mobile account. By allowing the transfer of funds between wallets, handsets effectively become mobile bank accounts.
Related Party A related party defines party or party role linked to a specific entity.
