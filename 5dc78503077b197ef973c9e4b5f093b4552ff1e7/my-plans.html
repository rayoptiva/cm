My Plan

Open the subscriptions page by clicking Subscriptions on the left navigation.

  1. Search by Account ID or BAN.
    • Click the advanced filter () to search by specific options.
  2. Filter your search by clicking the following filter icons:

    • Mobile
    • Landline
    • Ethernet
    • IPTV
  3. Click My Plan in the center of the selected subscription card to open My Plans.

View the following usage information in the My Plan section:

Data

  • Click Data to view data usage details.
    • Click View More to view more data usage details.
  • Click Buy Data to buy more data for the subscription.

SMS

  • Click SMS to view call details.
    • Click View More to view detailed SMS usage details.
  • Click Recharge to recharge the subscription.

Calls

  • Click Calls to view call details.
    • Click View More to view detailed call usage details.
  • Click Recharge to recharge the subscription.

Flex

View Calls

Click the Calls tab to view calls and view details for voice bundle usage.

View Data

Click the Data tab to view data details and view details for data bundle usage.
